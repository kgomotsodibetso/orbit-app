04
Logo Usage
The mark must always have sufficient contrast with its background. Do not alter the colour of individual spines, stretch the mark, or place it on backgrounds that obscure the spine detail.
Do
Use the full-colour mark on a clean, high-contrast background.
Don't
Do not stretch, squash, or distort the mark proportions.
Do
Use the dark-background variant on Slate and Ink backgrounds.
Don't
Do not place the mark on busy gradients or low-contrast backgrounds.
